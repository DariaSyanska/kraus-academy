About The Course

Everything you need to confidently enter the profession and create real client projects.

We Create History

This course is designed for anyone who wants to quickly and confidently master design from scratch. You’ll get clear explanations, practical assignments, and a structured learning path that helps you build skills and create professional projects.

The lessons are organized step by step, without overload, so you can see real results as you progress through the course.

Who It’s For

This course is ideal for beginner designers, students, freelancers, and anyone who wants to develop visual thinking and learn to create modern, high-quality projects without unnecessary complexity.

  • Beginners starting from zero
  • Students looking to build practical skills
  • Freelancers who want to expand their services
  • Anyone interested in design and creative fields

What It’s For

The course helps you gain practical skills, understand key design principles, build your first portfolio pieces, and confidently move toward a professional level or new career opportunities.

  • To learn real design tools and workflows
  • To understand how composition and color work
  • To create strong portfolio projects
  • To prepare for freelance work or a design-related career

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the course and how it will help you develop your skills.

Who is this course for?

This course is designed for beginners who want to start from scratch, as well as intermediate learners looking to solidify their foundational skills and learn best practices.

What’s included in the course?

You will get lifetime access to video tutorials, downloadable checklists, practical assignments after each module, and access to our private community chat.

How long will it take to complete the course?

The course is self-paced, so you can progress according to your schedule. On average, it takes about 6–8 weeks to complete with consistent practice.

What skills will I gain?

By the end of the course, you will master the core tools of the trade, learn how to build real-world projects, and develop a professional workflow that saves time.

Do I need any prior experience?

No prior experience is required! We start with the absolute basics and gradually move toward more complex topics. All you need is a computer and a desire to learn.

Will I get a certificate?

Yes! Upon successful completion of all modules and the final project, you will receive a digital certificate that you can add to your LinkedIn profile or resume.
